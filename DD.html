Dimension Drift

Dimension Drift is a GMTK game jam that I took part in, the theme of which was 'Joined Together'. I was the designer for the project and had ideas on how and where the mechanics should be used, I also acted as the level designer for the project and designed all levels.

Here are some of the skills I learned during the project:

  • Time Management
  • Co-ordinate with my team members on the vision of the game.
  • Choose the color palette for the game.
  • Design all levels for the game.
