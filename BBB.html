Bish Bash Bosh

Bish Bash Bosh is a multiplayer game where upto four player compete to survive in different game modes. The last person standing wins the map.

I worked as a junior designer, level designer and a QA tester for the game and I reported to my team lead and also helped in other departments when nessecary. My main priority was to design and playtest a game mode called egg game, the goal of which was to collect as many eggs as possible to your relative basket. I made maps for the game mode and also hed to test those maps and other game maps made by my peers.

Here were some of my responsibilities:

  • Showcase a pitch to lectureres about game idea #1.
  • Communicate with team about the games' vision and direction.
  • Design and prototype a game mode.
  • Blockout and develop maps for the game mode.
  • Playtest and bugfind other maps.
