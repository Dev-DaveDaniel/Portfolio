Colosseum

The goal of this project was to make a TF2 KOTH roman colosseum inspired map using the hammer engine. I thought that the agressive style of a colosseum was perfect for a FPS. Here was my process of building a FPS map over the span of six weeks.

  • Design documentation of the map
  • Learn the basics of a new engine in the span of a week
  • Research other KOTH maps to find similar patterns to help design the layout
  • Design and blockout a colosseum inspired KOTH layout
  • Playtest and re-iteration of design
  • Review of feedback
