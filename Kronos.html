Kronos

Kronos is a top down shooter that I had to design mechanics and a map for in unreal engine 4. I decided to make my main character the greek god Kronos, the mechanics that were developed for the project was time control and guard wall

Goal: The goal was to make a mechanic suitable for a top-down shooter and design a level.

Inspirations: I was heavily inspired by the game Hades and also the greek god Kronos.

What have I learned?

  • How to design and document a top-down shooter level.
  • How to tie in characters with use of story and setting.
  • How to use affordance in a 3D space.
  • Learn to use the cinematic tools to make a trailer.

    • What are my thoughts on the project?

    The only mechanic that I wasn't able to impliment was the fireball, I think the addition of this mechanic would have had some interesting gameplay. Overall I belive this project went in the direction that I expected it to go, I had a lot of fun coming up with the mechanics and designing them and wondeing about the impact of it on the game. This was my first attemp at a design document and it covered all basis in terms of documentation.
